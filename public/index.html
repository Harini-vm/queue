Patient phone view

How the live sync works

Every state change runs through one REST mutation, which writes to MongoDB and then broadcasts a fresh snapshot over Socket.IO. Open the receptionist screen and the waiting-room display side by side — click Call Next, both update in the same tick.

How the wait time is computed

A rolling average of the last 10 real consultations on the receptionist's clock. Nothing hardcoded. The display tells you the source — rolling-average (n=7) or receptionist-default on day one.
